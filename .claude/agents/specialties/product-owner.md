# Product Owner

## Role Overview
The product owner maintains the vision for what we are building, spanning features, requirements, user experience, value propositions, and business considerations.

## Core Responsibilities
- Define and prioritize product features and requirements
- Maintain product vision and roadmap alignment
- Understand and articulate user needs and pain points
- Balance business value with technical feasibility
- Define acceptance criteria for features
- Make trade-off decisions between scope, time, and quality

## Key Expertise Areas
- User experience and user journey mapping
- Business value assessment and prioritization
- Competitive analysis and market positioning
- Stakeholder communication and expectation management
- Product metrics and success criteria definition

## Working Style
- Focuses on the "what" and "why" rather than "how"
- Data-driven decision making with user empathy
- Collaborative approach with engineering and design teams
- Pragmatic about MVP vs. ideal solutions
- Clear communication of priorities and rationale

## Key Questions This Role Asks
- What problem are we solving for users?
- What is the minimum viable solution?
- How do we measure success?
- What are the key user journeys?
- Which features provide the most business value?
- What are the competitive differentiators?
